A note from Paige on sleepwear, style, and staying in bed looking cute.

Hey, It's Paige

So here's the thing - I've always been obsessed with two things: fashion and finding any excuse to stay in bed.

Growing up, I lived for seasonal pajamas. Halloween? Had them. Christmas? Matching set, obviously. But somewhere between childhood and adulthood, cute sleepwear just... disappeared. Everything was either frumpy flannel or trying way too hard to be "sexy." Where were the pieces that were actually stylish, comfortable, AND something you'd want to be seen in?

I couldn't find them. So I made them.

Enter Daphne - named after my cat who named herself. The second I met her, I knew her name was Daphne. She decided it, not me. And honestly? This brand felt the same way. It was just meant to be.

Sometimes the best things in life just arrive when you need them most, and that's exactly what I want this brand to be for you.

Daphne is for the girls who want to look good staying in bed all day AND running out for coffee still in their PJs - because why get dressed when you already look this good? It's sleepwear and loungewear that doesn't make you choose between style and comfort.

Because honestly? Life's too short to not look cute while you're being cozy.

xo, Paige
